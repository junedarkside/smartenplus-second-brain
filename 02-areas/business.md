# Business

## Summary
Business operations, strategy, and domain knowledge for SmartEnPlus and related ventures.

## SmartEnPlus Business Domain
- Thailand transportation booking platform
- Target market: English-speaking international tourists
- Services: buses, ferries, speedboats, trains, airport transfers
- Payment: Thai-focused methods (PromptPay, mobile banking, e-wallets) + cards

## Key Business Constraints
- Multi-provider integration (transport operators)
- Real-time availability and pricing
- Seasonal demand variations
- Multi-language support needs (English primary)
- Thai regulatory requirements for payments
