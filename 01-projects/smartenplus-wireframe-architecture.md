# SmartenPlus — Full Platform Wireframe Architecture
## Thailand Travel Infrastructure Platform

---

# 1. Platform Vision

## Core Product Concept

SmartenPlus is not:
- a generic OTA
- a transport booking utility
- a travel blog
- an activity marketplace

SmartenPlus is:

> **Thailand Travel Infrastructure Platform**

The platform helps travelers:
- move around Thailand
- discover destinations
- explore experiences
- understand routes
- travel confidently

Core emotional value:

```txt
Explore Thailand Easily
```

---

# 12. Mobile UX Strategy

## Mobile Principles

Use:
- horizontal snap scrolling
- sticky search
- large touch targets
- image-first discovery
- floating CTA patterns

Avoid:
- dense desktop grids
- overloaded filters
- tiny buttons
